Privacy Policy

We value your trust and transparency in how we handle your data.

Effective Date: October 25, 2025 | Website: VolhardConsulting.com

1. Introduction

Volhard Consulting ("we," "our," "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, interact with our content, or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Personal Information: Information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email, or company details via forms or inquiries.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected through cookies, pixels, and analytics tools to understand how you interact with our site.
  • Marketing and Retargeting Data: We use tracking technologies (LinkedIn Insight Tag, Meta Pixel, and Google Analytics) to measure engagement and display relevant advertising on other platforms.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Improve website functionality, design, and performance.
  • Understand user behavior for product and marketing optimization.
  • Deliver personalized ads across the web (retargeting).
  • Communicate updates, insights, and relevant offers when you consent.

4. Tracking Technologies

We use:

  • LinkedIn Insight Tag for anonymous conversion tracking and audience analytics.
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel for remarketing and measuring campaign effectiveness.
  • Google Analytics to analyze web traffic and improve user experience.

These tools may use cookies or similar technologies to track interactions across websites. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. However, anonymized behavioral data may be shared with trusted analytics or advertising partners (LinkedIn, Meta, Google) strictly for performance measurement and optimization.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above or as required by law.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Ask for correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of targeted advertising via your browser settings or the following:
    • LinkedIn Ad Settings
    • Facebook Ad Preferences
    • Google Ads Settings

8. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure hosting environments to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Effective Date."
